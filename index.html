Information

Normal - a normal condition of an eye.

Mild - The initial stage of DR. Micro aneurysms(small swelling) appear that may cause leak of fluid into the retina.

Moderate - The progressive stage that cause swell and distortion of blood vessels that are connected to retina(for blood and nourishment).

Severe - The severe condition of DR where many blood vessels blocked making less supply of blood to retina(new blood vessels appear).

Proliferative - The advanced stage of DR caused by blockage of tiny blood vessels(that grow inside). It damage the retina.
